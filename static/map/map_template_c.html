  • Mapped Status* Mapping a school refers to establishing its location and connectivity requirements.

    1. Geographically - The school has identification, location, and infrustructural data only.

    2. Partially - The school is geograpahically mapped and the required network baandwidth has been calculated but lacks the parameters needed for determining the most affordable middle- mile or last- mile option

    3. Fully - The school is geographically mapped and all the data needed to determine the required bandwidth, the most affordable middle- and last- mile solutions and projected costs.
